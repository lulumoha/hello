A program to greet the planet.

This page is an attempt to greet everyone on earth using our various human languages. We are interested to see how long our list can become.

  • English:Hello,world!
  • Spanish:Hola,mondo!
  • Japanese:Konnichiwa,sekai!
  • French:Bonjour,monde!
  • Kinyarwanda:Mwirwe,isi!
  • Somali:Salam,adun!
